### Question 8

Repeat Exercise 7, but give the equivalent hexadecimal escape.

### Solution

|     | Escape Sequence | Decimal Code Point | Hex Code Point | Hex Escape Sequence |
|:---:|:---------------:|:------------------:|:--------------:|:-------------------:|
| (a) |       `\b`      |          8         |       8        |        `\x8`        |
| (b) |       `\n`      |         10         |       A        |        `\xA`        |
| (c) |       `\r`      |         13         |       D        |        `\xD`        |
| (d) |       `\t`      |          9         |       9        |        `\x9`        |
